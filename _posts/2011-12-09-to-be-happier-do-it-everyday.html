Do it Twice a day.  when you weak up and before your sleep, and write down it (20 minutes). and using the a hour to do what you like (ex. music game)

1. image: (then write down)

a. what do you want to be?

b. how hard process is it to be?

c. image the peak experiences?

2. face, accept, focus on the positive side  (then write down)

a. face and accept the trouble in your life.

b. focus on the positive side.

c. think the solution to it

d. add the solution to your schedule and do it.

3. appreciate:  (then write down)

a. family

b. friend and teacher

c. your memory

d. peak experience
