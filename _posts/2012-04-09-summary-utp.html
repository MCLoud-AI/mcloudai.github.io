using the different formal method to describe different level of software enginerring.

 

three main method:

1.  denotational semantics -  denote everything as predicate.

no matter the specification, design, program.....

then you can use the predicate calculus to verify the character.

you can derive the algebraic law (equation or inequation)from the predicate.

In utp, it define the basic program unit using predicate.  and all of this are monotonic .

2.  algebraic semantics

providing equations and inequations for comparison, trans-formation a nd optimisation of designs and programs.

3.  operational semantics

describing individual steps of a possible mechanical implementation.

the mainly work is define the pattern transition.

 

at last, the three semantics must be consistency.

beside, the other extension must be interest:

timing, probability and parallel
