One server, one PC, tools NC

1. Listen on the server

nc -l 4444 (for tcp)

nc -l -u 4444 (for udp)

2. Connect from a PC

echo aaa | nc -u xxxx port

3. scan

nc -z -v hostname 4444 (for tcp)

nc -z -v -u hostname 4444 (for udp)

one port could listen on TCP and UDP together.
