Photo

  • iPhone to iMac: Image Capture with wired connection
  • iMac to iPhone/iPad: My Photo Steam, (iTunes can transfer, but you can not delete and edit photo on iPhone)
  • AirDrop: can choose arbitrary photo to transfer. (same as moive)

Music

  • iMac to iPhone: iTunes (apple cloud music will make downloaded music disapper, when change iCloud account)
