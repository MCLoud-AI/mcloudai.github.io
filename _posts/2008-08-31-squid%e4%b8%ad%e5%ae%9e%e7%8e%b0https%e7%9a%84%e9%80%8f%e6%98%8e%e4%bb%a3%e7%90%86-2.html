1.生成测试用的证书：

openssl req -new -keyout /etc/squid/key.pem -nodes -x509 -days 365 -out /etc/squid/cert.pem

2. 在squid.conf相应地方添加

https_port 443 cert=/etc/squid/cert.pem /etc/squid/key.pem transparent
